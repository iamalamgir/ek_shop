    Planning and Implementation

    Enjoy your finest shopping experience with ekShop!

    Make your shopping experience smooth like never before with ekShop, the world's first integrated assisted rural e-Commerce platform. ekShop incorporates all the top e-Commerce, payments, and logistic players into one platform and acts as a national e-Commerce infrastructure backbone. With a continuous effort to escalate in a global e-commerce market, a search in its platform can compare similar products drawn from multiple e-Commerce platforms in a single window, increasing the chances of a consumer finding a preferred outcome, in contrast to the potential limitations of choosing in a particular site. The same system enables producers and retailers to sell their products to the market participants as a single-entry point. They can go to the government-owned union digital centre or virtually enter as a vendor into the platform and upload their product to ekShop. It automatically is available to ekShop's partners' eCommerce websites.

    Launched by Aspire to Innovate (a2i) Programme of the Bangladesh government and United Nations Development Programme (UNDP) in 2018, its ecosystem facilitates a robust national and international chain of goods transactions, ensuring the utmost financial safety between entrepreneurs, producers and consumers.

    In a bid to enrich its capacity, ekShop has facilitated many cottages, micro, small and medium enterprises (CMSMEs) - led mainly by women entrepreneurs - with open commerce platforms such as Anondomela, Joyeeta, WE, eMARKET of SME Foundation and Bangladesh Small and Cottage Industries Corporation (BSCIC) and Digital Haat.

    In addition, its initiative p-commerce like Phone-e-nittoponno (333-5) and 'Food for Nation' platform eased thousands of registered farmers and local businesses to post their ads for Agri products at local market prices, leaving them not wary about middlemen and selling perishables items. ekShop has expanded its chain of services internationally to Xiaomi Mobile, Malaysia, Singapore, Nepal, Yemen, South Sudan, Turkey, Colombia, Gulf region, North America and Europe .
